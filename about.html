About Astra Biocare

Empowering health through science-first pharmaceutical innovation

Astra Biocare Pvt Ltd is a cutting-edge pharmaceutical company dedicated to bringing affordable, high-quality, and innovative healthcare solutions to patients across India. Founded with a mission to bridge the gap between advanced medical science and accessible patient care, we specialize in women's wellness, intimate care, respiratory health, and specialty therapeutics.

Our Mission

To deliver science-first pharmaceutical formulations that are safe, effective, and accessible to every patient. We combine rigorous research, WHO-GMP certified manufacturing, and transparent communication to ensure healthcare professionals and patients can make informed decisions with confidence.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted pharmaceutical partner by setting new standards in product quality, ingredient transparency, and patient-centric innovation. We envision a healthcare ecosystem where every prescription is backed by complete composition disclosure and traceable sourcing.
